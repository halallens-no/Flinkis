MyRoutine helps kids build healthy daily habits through a fun, interactive checklist with reward points!

FEATURES:
- Daily routine checklists grouped by time of day (Morning, School, Afternoon, Evening, Bedtime)
- Reward points system with milestones and badges
- Weekly progress view to track completion
- Printable routine sheets for the fridge or school
- Multiple child profiles with individual routines
- Three fun themes: Adventure (blue), Sparkle (pink), Nature (green)
- Optimized for Samsung Galaxy Tab and Android phones

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Choose a fun theme (Adventure, Sparkle, or Nature)
2. Enter your child's name and pick an avatar
3. Start checking off daily routines to earn points!
4. Unlock badges and milestones as you go

Parents can customize routines, add their own activities, and manage multiple children. Print weekly sheets so kids can check off tasks even without the tablet!

Perfect for families who want to:
- Build consistent morning and bedtime routines
- Encourage responsibility and independence
- Track homework and reading habits
- Make chores fun with reward points

PRIVACY:
- Fully offline - no internet required
- No ads, no tracking, no data collection
- All data stays on your device
